2005 Founded

More Than a School — A Community of Scholars

Progressive International Standard School was established in 2005 with a vision to provide world-class education accessible to every child. Our holistic approach combines rigorous academics with arts, sports, and character development.

We are accredited by the national education board and recognized for consistently producing top-performing students in national examinations. Our alumni include professors, engineers, doctors, entrepreneurs, and public servants across the globe.
